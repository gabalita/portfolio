Fluenteam Language Platform

Feb 2021 - May 2021

During the pandemic, I took an interest in French and began studying the language from scratch. After a couple months, I realized Duolingo wasn't challenging enough for intermediate speakers. With this idea in mind, I wanted to see if I could create a platform that better engaged intermediate English speakers in Spanish speaking countries.

Fluenteam was truly a passion project. While making the website, I performed all parts of the project: product research and competitor analysis, interviews with learners, MVP design and outline, graphic and UX/UI design, learning illustrations with Procreate, web development, content creation, social media marketing on Instagram and Facebook, email outreach with MailChimp, and customer response. Although I no longer operate Fluenteam, the project gave me a chance to experience the life cycle of an idea, and in the process I tutored hundreds of students.
